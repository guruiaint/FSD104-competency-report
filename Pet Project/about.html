About:

Fashionable Furry Friends was established in 2021 after a group of strangers that were locked down in an Air BnB for 6 months due to the pandemic decided they all wanted to follow their pashion. Amazingly, all five of those peoples' pashions happened to be grooming pets. They will endeavor to provide every furry friend with the absolute best care and pampering they can provide. So thank you for stopping by and trusting Fabulous Furry Friends with all your pet grooming needs.
